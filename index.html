How it works

Three minutes from install to insight.

No signup. No account. No data uploaded anywhere. Caza reads only what Zillow already sends to your browser.

Step 01

Install from the Chrome Web Store

One click. The extension is unobfuscated, free, and asks for the minimum permissions Chrome allows.

Step 02

Open any Zillow search

Caza activates only on Zillow listing pages. Nothing changes anywhere else in your browser.

Step 03

See demand scores instantly

Every listing shows a 0–100 score. Click for the full breakdown. That’s it.

FAQ

Questions, before you ask.

Does Caza work on every Zillow listing?
It works on every active for-sale listing in the US. Off-market homes and rentals aren’t supported yet.
Will using Caza affect my Zillow account?
No. Caza reads only the public data Zillow already sends to your browser. It doesn’t scrape, doesn’t automate clicks, and doesn’t touch your account or login.
What does the PDF report look like?
Cover photo, listing photos, full address, demand score with rationale, save and view trends, days on market, and price history. Print-ready, one click from any listing page.
How is the demand score calculated?
It blends three Zillow-native signals: total saves, recent view velocity, and days on market relative to the local average. The weighting adjusts per market so a hot home in Austin doesn’t get compared to one in San Francisco.
